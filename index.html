Arrow Head

Those who made you bleed
Thinking they are as strong as a stead
Just feel happy, from them you're freed
And god's judgment won't be sweet

You're care for others is vast
And your love will forever last
Was it a spell that you did cast?
Because i loved you so much, and fast

The cuteness meter breaks when it sees your face
You and beauty are connected tightly with a lace
When i saw you, i felt like getting hit with a mace
How lucky should one be to meet the best one in human race?

Angelic voice with no halo floating
Just having it in my ear is mesmerizing
Hope, determination, love, this voice keeps bringing
It's not enough thanking god day and night for this blessing

Personality like nothing else
In that kind heart nothing is false
Only a pure and clean sense
I would fight all my life because your heart is immense

Having you is more like a dream
It's not everyday that you see someone that has steam
You make me laugh even harder than a meme
You are more important than a cake's top cream

Everyone's core is made of lead
Yours is made of gold and that cannot be spread
I know for a fact that when we meet i will be red
Because...i got hit with a cupid's arrow head

To:The best person alive <3
From: Secret admirer
