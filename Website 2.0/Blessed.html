Blessed

It's true, most of the time we jest
But i know deep down you feel messed
To me, you opened your chest
And for the love of god, stop thinking that you're a pest
I will sacrifice anything because making you happy is my quest
And i will torture myself if i ever find out you are depressed
You're like the X mark, hiding the treasure chest
Or maybe the loot hidden deep down the monster nest
Eitherway you may have guessed
That, indeed, you are the best
And from god, you were blessed
